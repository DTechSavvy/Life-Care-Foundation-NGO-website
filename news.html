Sarah's Journey: From Village to CEO

Location: Rural Maharashtra, India

The Beginning

Growing up in a remote village with limited resources, Sarah faced numerous challenges in pursuing her dreams...

The Transformation

  • Quality education and vocational training
  • Business management skills
